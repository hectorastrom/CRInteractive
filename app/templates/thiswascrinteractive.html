This Was CRInteractive

January 27th, 2023

Dear CRInteractive community,

On November 28th, 2023, users trying to log into their accounts encountered an unexpected error message that blocked access to their accounts. This was because our database provider suspended the service that hosted our database since we launched. Consequently, all data logged over the last 18 months was lost and cannot be restored.

Sadly, CRInteractive will also be suspending its service as of today. We are delighted with the personal and community-wide impact the website had during its service. Thank you for joining us on this journey!

If you’d like to read more about the specifics about what it was like creating and maintaining CRInteractive, we’ve written a more in depth post here.

All the best,

Albert and Hector Astrom

Creators of CRInteractive
